Alibaba Feedflow

Feedflow is a unique social - shopping feed that transform lightweight product listings into snackable posts optimized for other social apps and feeds. We leverage machine learning to improve the quality of supplier-generated content in order to help them selling more products and keeping the existing buyers. As the usage grows the News Feed will eventually serve as a standalone private networking gadget for Alibaba social shopping.

Zynga's Ayakashi 2

Ayakashi 2 is a mobile RPG card collective game that's heavily influenced by Japanese myth. The key mechanism in Ayakashi 2: Characters and Monsters are cards, which are collectible. They can be enhanced, evolved and used for battle. There is also a central story running throughout the game, though it is as yet incomplete. Various statistics govern the player's ability to explore the storyline and use their cards for battle, while events provide additional opportunities for card collection.

Zynga's Clumsy Ninja

As featured in Google Play's Best Games of 2014. Clumsy Ninja is the most hapless ninja ever to grace a touchscreen. Train him, throw him, tickle him, and even tie balloons to him. He can sense, feel, move, and react uniquely every time. Everything users do will make Clumsy Ninja more skillful, and help him find his missing friend Kira.
